Maemo

Governance, Risk & Compliance Platform
Comprehensive GRC Solution for South African Organizations

Executive Summary

Maemo is a comprehensive Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC) platform designed specifically for South African organizations. Built on modern .NET 8 and Angular technologies, Maemo provides a unified solution for managing documents, compliance, risk, audits, and non-conformance reporting.

The platform addresses critical business needs including POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act) compliance, AGSA (Auditor-General of South Africa) audit readiness, BBBEE certification management, National Archives file plan alignment, and comprehensive audit trail capabilities.

Maemo is designed to support compliance with South African regulations and international standards:

POPIA AGSA ISO 9001:2015 ISO 27001 NARSA BBBEE National Archives

POPIA Compliance Features

  • PII Classification: Categorize documents by personal information type (Personal Info, Special Personal Info, Children's Info, Financial, Health)
  • Retention Management: Set and track retention periods for personal information
  • Access Trail: Complete audit log of who accessed personal information and when
  • Inventory Reports: Comprehensive personal information inventory for compliance reporting
  • Retention Expiry Alerts: Dashboard widgets showing documents approaching retention expiry

AGSA Audit Readiness

  • Evidence Packs: Generate comprehensive audit evidence reports (PDF) including:
    • Document metadata & versions
    • Complete business audit logs
    • Approval history
    • Linked NCRs and Risks
    • All supporting documentation
  • Audit Trail: Immutable business audit logs for all critical operations
  • Version Control: Complete document version history with timestamps and comments

Key Business Use Cases

1. Document Lifecycle Management

Scenario: An organization needs to manage policy documents, procedures, and certificates through their entire lifecycle.

Solution: Maemo provides:

  • Draft → Review → Approval → Active → Archive workflow
  • Version control with complete history
  • Review date tracking and reminders
  • Approval workflow with comments and rejection reasons
  • Template library for quick document creation

2. POPIA Compliance Management

Scenario: An organization must demonstrate POPIA compliance and maintain a personal information inventory.

Solution: Maemo enables:

  • Classification of all documents containing personal information
  • Automatic tracking of access to personal information
  • Retention period management with expiry alerts
  • Comprehensive inventory reports for regulatory submission
  • Access trail reports showing who accessed what and when

3. AGSA Audit Preparation

Scenario: An organization needs to prepare evidence packs for AGSA audits.

Solution: Maemo generates:

  • Complete audit evidence packs (PDF) with one click
  • All document versions, audit logs, approvals, and linked items
  • Professional formatted reports ready for submission
  • Immutable audit trail demonstrating compliance

4. Non-Conformance & Corrective Action

Scenario: An organization needs to track non-conformances and manage corrective actions.

Solution: Maemo provides:

  • NCR creation with severity classification
  • Root cause analysis documentation
  • Corrective action planning and tracking
  • Linkage to related risks and documents
  • Status workflow from Open → In Progress → Closed

5. Risk Register Management

Scenario: An organization needs to maintain a comprehensive risk register.

Solution: Maemo enables:

  • Risk identification and categorization
  • Inherent and residual risk assessment
  • 5x5 risk matrix (Likelihood × Impact)
  • Control documentation
  • Linkage to NCRs and related documents

6. BBBEE Certificate Management

Scenario: An organization needs to track BBBEE certificates and ensure timely renewal.

Solution: Maemo provides:

  • Certificate document storage
  • Level classification (1-8)
  • Expiry date tracking
  • Dashboard alerts for expiring certificates
  • Expiry reports for proactive management
