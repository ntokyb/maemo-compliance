What is Maemo Compliance

Maemo Compliance is a platform for South African organisations to manage ISO-aligned quality, environment, health & safety, and POPIA-related document and audit workflows in one place.

Who built it

Maemo Compliance is built and operated by Codist (Pty) Ltd.
