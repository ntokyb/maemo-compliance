# Audit Evidence Register

## Purpose

This register is used to document and track all evidence collected during audits conducted in accordance with Auditor-General of South Africa (AGSA) standards. It ensures proper chain of custody, integrity, and accessibility of audit evidence.

## Scope

This register applies to all evidence collected during:

- Financial audits
- Performance audits
- Compliance audits
- Information systems audits
- Special investigations
