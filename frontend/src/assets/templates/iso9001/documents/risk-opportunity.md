# Risk and Opportunity Management Procedure

## Purpose

This procedure establishes the requirements for:

- Identifying risks and opportunities that could affect the Quality Management System
- Assessing risks and opportunities
- Planning and implementing actions to address risks and opportunities
- Monitoring and reviewing risks and opportunities
- Integrating risk-based thinking into QMS processes

## Scope

This procedure applies to all risks and opportunities related to:

- Achievement of quality objectives
- Customer satisfaction
- Product and service conformity
- QMS effectiveness
- Organisational context and interested parties

## Responsibilities

### Top Management
- Overall responsibility for risk and opportunity management
- Approving risk treatment plans
- Allocating resources for risk mitigation

### Quality Manager
- Coordinating risk and opportunity management processes
- Maintaining the risk register
- Reporting on risks and opportunities

### Process Owners
- Identifying risks and opportunities in their processes
- Assessing and treating risks
- Monitoring risk mitigation actions

### All Personnel
- Reporting risks and opportunities
- Participating in risk assessments
- Implementing risk mitigation actions

## Procedure

### 4.1 Risk and Opportunity Identification

Risks and opportunities are identified through:

- Context analysis (internal and external factors)
- Interested party analysis
- Process analysis
- Internal and external audits
- Management review
- Customer feedback
- Nonconformity analysis
- Data analysis and trends

### 4.2 Risk Assessment

1. **Risk Analysis**
   - Likelihood: Probability of occurrence (Low, Medium, High)
   - Impact: Severity of consequences (Low, Medium, High)
   - Risk Level: Combination of likelihood and impact

2. **Risk Evaluation**
   - Risks are evaluated against risk criteria
   - Risk levels are assigned:
     - Low: Acceptable, monitor
     - Medium: Mitigate or monitor
     - High: Mitigate or avoid
     - Critical: Immediate action required

### 4.3 Opportunity Assessment

1. **Opportunity Analysis**
   - Potential benefits
   - Feasibility
   - Resource requirements
   - Alignment with quality objectives

2. **Opportunity Evaluation**
   - Opportunities are prioritized
   - High-value opportunities are selected for action

### 4.4 Risk Treatment

1. **Risk Treatment Options**
   - Avoid: Eliminate the risk
   - Mitigate: Reduce likelihood or impact
   - Transfer: Share or transfer the risk
   - Accept: Accept the risk with monitoring

2. **Risk Treatment Plan**
   - Selected treatment option
   - Actions to be taken
   - Responsible person(s)
   - Target completion date
   - Success criteria

3. **Implementation**
   - Risk treatment actions are implemented
   - Progress is monitored
   - Effectiveness is verified

### 4.5 Opportunity Action Planning

1. **Opportunity Action Plan**
   - Actions to exploit the opportunity
   - Responsible person(s)
   - Resource requirements
   - Target completion date
   - Expected benefits

2. **Implementation**
   - Opportunity actions are implemented
   - Progress is monitored
   - Benefits are measured

### 4.6 Risk Register

A risk register is maintained documenting:

- Risk/Opportunity description
- Category (process, product, system, external)
- Likelihood and impact
- Risk/Opportunity level
- Treatment/Action plan
- Responsible person
- Status
- Review date

### 4.7 Monitoring and Review

1. Risks and opportunities are reviewed:
   - Regularly (quarterly or as specified)
   - When new risks/opportunities are identified
   - When context changes
   - At management review

2. Review includes:
   - Status of risk treatment actions
   - Effectiveness of risk mitigation
   - New or changed risks
   - New opportunities

3. Risk register is updated

### 4.8 Integration with QMS

Risk-based thinking is integrated into:

- Quality planning
- Process design and control
- Internal audits
- Management review
- Corrective action
- Continual improvement

## Records

The following records are maintained:

- Risk register
- Risk assessment records
- Risk treatment plans
- Opportunity action plans
- Risk review records
- Risk monitoring data
