# POPIA Data Breach Notification Template

## Purpose

This template is used to document and report data breaches in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) No. 4 of 2013. It ensures that all breaches are properly recorded, assessed, and reported to the Information Regulator and affected data subjects as required by law.

## Scope

This template applies to all personal information breaches involving:

- Unauthorized access to personal information
- Loss of personal information
- Destruction of personal information
- Alteration of personal information
- Unauthorized disclosure of personal information
